Visit Our Pro Shop

Whether you’re gearing up for wakeboarding, surfing, or just spending time on the water, our Pro Shops have what you need. From top-name boards and safety gear to ropes, apparel, and accessories, the WakeShop offers a curated selection and expert guidance to ensure you’re ready for adventure. Drop by during your visit to the marina or showroom — our goal is to help make every outing more fun and more memorable.

What to Expect at Our Pro Shops

A curated selection of wakeboarding / watersports gear & accessories

Boards, ropes, vests, surf gear — things you need for fun on the water.

Expert Advice

Staff familiar with both gear and boating, able to help you choose what works best. (Because gear does make a difference.)

Convenience

Because the WakeShop is part of many of the marinas/showrooms, you can shop gear when you buy a boat, bring your boat in for service, or stop by when you’re out enjoying your time on the water.
