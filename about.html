WHO WE ARE

History & Humble Beginnings

Founded by the Singleton family in the late 1980s, Singleton Marine has always been driven by a simple promise: to treat every customer like part of the family. With multiple locations across Alabama, Georgia, and South Carolina, we strive to deliver not just boats, but memories—through quality, integrity, and service.

Our Mission & Values

Respect & Transparency

Being honest in what we sell, how we service your boat, and what you should expect long term.

Quality Craftsmanship

Only the best brands, the most reliable parts, and technicians who care about doing the job right.

Lifelong Relationships

We’re here after the sale: for maintenance, upgrades, storage, or just helping you explore your next boat adventure.

Looking Ahead

Growth & Community

Though we are now part of OneWater Marine, which was formed in 2014 through the merger of Singleton Marine and Legendary Marine, our foundation remains the same: local commitment and passion for boating. Over the years, we’ve expanded reach and resources but never lost the values of hard work, attention to detail, and putting customers first.
